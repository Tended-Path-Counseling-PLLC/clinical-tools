15-Minute Prospective Client — Clinician Working Document
Date
Time
Prospective Client (initials or pseudonym)
01 Opening Frame
Script: "I have about 15 minutes with you today. My goal is to learn a little about what's bringing you in and make sure we're a good fit. I'll ask a few questions, and you'll have time to ask me anything too."
02 What's Bringing Them In
What's prompting you to reach out for therapy right now — what's feeling most pressing?
How long has this been going on, and have you tried addressing it before?
03 History & Current Context
Have you been in therapy before? If so, what worked — and what didn't?
Are you currently seeing any other mental health providers, or taking medication for mental health?
04 Logistics & Fit
Are you looking for in-person sessions, telehealth, or either?
Do you have a sense of how frequently you'd want to meet?
What insurance are you using, or are you looking at private pay?
05 Safety Screen
Are you having any thoughts of harming yourself or others right now?
⚑ If yes — assess fully and document before proceeding with any fit discussion. Do not skip.
06 Their Questions + Clinical Impressions
What questions do you have for me about how I work or what to expect?
